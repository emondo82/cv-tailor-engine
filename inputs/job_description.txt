About the job
Be part of a global digital transformation

Our Services business is a fast‑growing, multi‑billion‑euro organization with bold ambitions. To support this growth, we are transforming how we work — from processes to digital platforms.

As an IT Business Analyst, you’ll play a key role in shaping our end‑to‑end front‑office digital solutions and helping the business scale globally.

You’ll work at the intersection of business, IT, and digital platforms, translating complex business needs into scalable, future‑proof solutions.

What You’ll Do

Act as a bridge between business stakeholders and IT teams, translating business needs into clear system requirements
Analyze and challenge requirements, identify gaps, and propose solution enhancements
Lead cross‑functional workshops to define as‑is and to‑be processes
Collaborate with internal teams and external IT partners across multiple countries
Contribute to large‑scale front‑office and services transformation initiatives
Support digital platforms such as Pricing, Quotation, and CPQ solutions
Ensure solutions follow reference models and scalable best practices
Take ownership of specific topics such as RPA, document management, UI/UX, or mobile solutions

What You Bring

University or Engineering degree (technical or business‑focused)
5+ years of experience in IT or business analysis roles
 SAP SD, CS, PS module technical support 3+ experience
Strong experience translating business requirements into functional or technical specifications
Exposure to front‑office or enterprise platforms (e.g. Salesforce, Oracle CPQ, Pricefx, Oracle ERP, or similar)
Experience working in complex, international environments
Strong analytical and problem‑solving skills
Confident communication skills with the ability to influence stakeholders at different levels
Fluency in English

Nice to have:

Experience in Services business, digital customer transformations, or global rollout programs
